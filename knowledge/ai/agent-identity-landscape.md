# Agent Identity Landscape (Q1 2026)

## The Core Problem

Non-human identities (NHIs) — service accounts, API tokens, bots, AI agents — outnumber human identities 40:1 to 500:1 in enterprises (96:1 in fintech per a16z). Growing 40%+ YoY. Most are "unbanked ghosts" with no credentials linking them to principals or liability.

- 78% of orgs have NO formal policy for creating or removing AI identities
- 92% aren't confident legacy IAM tools can manage agent identity risks
- One Identity predicts first major breach from an over-privileged AI agent in 2026
- "Identity dark matter" = unmanaged agents are fastest-growing source of invisible identity sprawl

## Standards Race (all converging Q1 2026)

| Standard | Backer | Scope | Status |
|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| **ERC-8004** | MetaMask, EF, Google, Coinbase | On-chain identity registry (ERC-721) | Mainnet Jan 29. v2 adds MCP+x402 |

| **a16z KYA** | a16z crypto | "Know Your Agent" for financial services | Framework published Mar 2026 |
| **World AgentKit** | World (Worldcoin) | ZK proofs of human backing via Orb biometrics + x402 | Active |
| **OWASP MCP Top 10** | OWASP | Agent security standard | v0.1 beta published |

Everyone's building agent identity simultaneously because everyone realized agents that act autonomously need identity infrastructure that doesn't exist yet. The parallel to early internet (no HTTPS, no OAuth, no PKI) is exact.

## Know Your Agent (KYA)

a16z coined KYA as the identity parallel to KYC. Central thesis: the bottleneck in the agent economy is shifting from intelligence to identity. Without verifiable credentials, agentic commerce cannot scale past trust boundaries.

## Adoption Data

- OECD AI company adoption: 8.7% (2023) → 14.2% (2024) → 20.2% (2025)
- Visa, Stripe, Mastercard all shipping agentic commerce products
- 85% of enterprises testing agents, only 5% in production (trust gap)

## Crypto's Structural Advantage

CryptoSlate thesis: crypto's real AI win is infrastructure (stablecoins, wallets, identity), not AI tokens. "The perfect user of programmable money is actually software." AI tokens struggle to hold value; lasting value comes from infra layers people actually use.
